\# Bear Shell Enclosure (3D Print)



\## Parts

\- Head shell (camera eye + LED eye + mouth speaker grille)

\- Ears (peg mounts)

\- Front body (screen cutout + pill cup opening)

\- Back service panel (screw-on)

\- Screen bezel lip

\- Cup drawer face/cover



\## Target Size

\- Height ~170mm

\- Width ~140mm

\- Depth ~110mm

\- Wall thickness ~3mm



\## Openings

\- Screen cutout: 85mm x 55mm

\- Pill cup opening: ~50mm x 25mm

\- Vent slots: 3mm x 20mm (6–8 slots)



\## Styling

Use large fillets (~10mm) to give a toy-like friendly bear look.
