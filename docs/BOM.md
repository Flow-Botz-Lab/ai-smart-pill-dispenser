\# Bill of Materials (BOM)



\## Compute + UI

\- Raspberry Pi 5 (8GB recommended)

\- 3.5" Touchscreen Display (Waveshare or similar)

\- microSD Card (64GB+)

\- NeoPixel 24 LED Ring (status indicator)



\## Dispensing Mechanism

\- NEMA 17 Stepper Motor (carousel indexing)

\- A4988 Stepper Driver (or DRV8825)

\- MG90S Micro Servo (sliding shutter gate)

\- IR Break Beam Sensor (pill drop confirmation)

\- Hall Effect Sensor + Magnet (homing / slot 1)



\## Power

\- 18650 Batteries x2 (or protected pack)

\- TP4056 Charging Module (or protected charger solution)

\- 5V Buck Converter / Regulator for Raspberry Pi

\- Power switch, wiring, connectors



\## Audio

\- 3W Mini Speaker

\- Small Audio Amplifier (optional)

\- Buzzer (optional)



\## Mechanical / Assembly

\- PLA/PETG Filament (~0.5kg prototype)

\- M3 Screws + Nuts Kit

\- Heat-set inserts (recommended)

\- Optional 608 bearing (smooth carousel)
